# Avigad, Mathematical Logic and Computation

## Prerequisites
(none)

## Notes
Author: Avigad. Propositional and first-order logic via natural
deduction and sequent calculus, completeness, computability, Gödel's
incompleteness, intuitionistic logic and type theory. Written with a
formalization sensibility (Avigad co-founded mathlib) — a natural
companion for doing the exercises in Lean.
