## Notes
Broad combinatorics — counting, designs, codes, Ramsey, extremal,
probabilistic method. van Lint & Wilson is the classic survey;
West is the modern narrative alternative. For deeper enumerative
work see `enumerative-combinatorics`; for graphs specifically see
`graph-theory`.
